**** Actions of student cheating in paper-based exams database

A team of researchers from the A group of researchers at the Hashemite University has prepared a cheating database. 
Photography was in one of the classrooms at the Faculty of Information Technology using a sensor camera Canon 70D. 
The sensor recorded 24 frames per second, and the image size was 1920x1080 pixels. 
The distance between the sensor and the recorded scene is approximately 3 meters. 
All videos are available in (.avi) file format.
There are 5 activities: exchanging exam papers, looking at a student’s exam paper, using a cheat sheet, using a cellular device, and not cheating. 
We recorded eight unique subjects: four female students and four male students. Each pair of subjects conducted five distinct cheating activities.
The total number of video sequences is 37.  





****Objective
    - Researchers can use this database to produce useful and influential scientific work in identifying cases of cheating, which can help address this problem.
